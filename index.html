The Story

The Fall

The town of Thornhaven was quiet at dusk. Lanterns flickered along cobblestone streets. Market stalls shuttered for the night. A bell tower stood watch over a place at peace.

Then the ground shook. Cracks split the cobblestone. Dark mist seeped up from below. The church bell rang on its own — and the earth broke open.

A hooded figure named Thelsicker fell through the rupture — past the town's foundation, past ancient stone, past forgotten ruins carved with glowing runes — into a darkness older than the world above.

Now trapped beneath 12 zones of escalating darkness, armed with nothing but a rusted sword, Thelsicker must fight through ancient guardians to awaken forgotten magic. Seven devastating spells lie dormant. Each boss defeated reclaims a fragment of power. But the deeper you descend, the more the darkness pushes back — and the way home grows further away.

The Lore

The World Beneath Thornhaven

Thornhaven was a quiet town built on the ruins of something ancient. When the rupture tore through its foundation, it revealed a vertical labyrinth — 12 zones of darkness, each ruled by a guardian who hoards a fragment of forgotten magic.

☤

The Rupture

No one knows what caused it. The earth cracked, the bell tower rang on its own, and dark mist seeped through the cobblestone. The town of Thornhaven split open in moments. Beneath the rubble lay something far older — tunnels and temples carved with runes that glowed faintly in the dark. The town was built on a seal. And the seal broke.

🔥

Dark Essence

The creatures below bleed a substance the old texts called Dark Essence — concentrated arcane power left behind when the ancient mages sealed themselves underground. Every enemy slain leaves behind a glowing orb of this essence. Thelsicker absorbs it to strengthen spells, fortify defenses, and push deeper into the abyss. The deeper you go, the richer the essence — and the more dangerous its source.

🔮

The Seven Spells

Each zone boss guards a fragment of power — one of seven lost spells that once belonged to the archmages who built the labyrinth. Mana Surge, Dark Harvest, Essence Storm, Shadow Clone, Time Warp, Void Pulse, and Dark Pact. Reclaim them all and you may have enough power to ascend.

⚖

The Artifacts

Hidden throughout the labyrinth are 8 ancient artifacts — relics of the mages who sealed this place. The Shadow Amulet dampens incoming damage. The Cursed Tome amplifies spell power. The Abyssal Crown regenerates health in darkness. Each one is hidden behind ability-gated secrets that reward exploration and backtracking.

☠

The Guardians

Twelve bosses stand between Thelsicker and the surface. The Awakening Guardian tests your blade. The Eldritch Horror warps reality itself. And at the deepest point, the Primordial One waits — the source of all dark essence, the being the ancient mages failed to destroy. Each guardian has multiple combat phases and unique attack patterns.

💡

Save Crystals

Scattered throughout the labyrinth are crystalline formations that pulse with residual arcane energy. These Save Crystals serve as checkpoints — touch one and its light intensifies, marking your position. When death comes, you return here. The crystals are the only mercy the labyrinth offers.

The Cast

Characters & Creatures

A dark wizard, an undead sword, and eight types of ancient horrors standing in your way.

Thelsicker

Dark Wizard — Protagonist

A hooded mage who arrived in Thornhaven seeking answers about a recurring dream — a voice calling from beneath the earth. When the rupture swallowed the town, Thelsicker fell into the labyrinth armed with nothing but a jagged, bone-hilted sword that seems to drink the dark.

Pallid skin, glowing magenta eyes, and a tattered cloak that billows even without wind. As spells are reclaimed from the bosses below, Thelsicker transforms from a desperate swordsman into an arcane force capable of bending shadow, time, and void.

Sword Combat 3-Hit Combo Wall Climb Dash Double Jump 7 Spells

The Enemies

Eight ancient creature types haunt the labyrinth. Each behaves differently — learn their patterns or be consumed.

Shadow Imp

Patrol & Chase

Small, purple creatures that patrol the upper zones. Fast but fragile — they charge on sight and swarm in groups.

Bone Golem

Patrol & Charge

Massive skeleton constructs assembled from the bones of fallen mages. Slow but devastating — they charge at full speed when they spot you.

Spectral Mage

Ranged & Kite

Translucent remnants of the ancient archmages, floating above the ground. They fire arcane bolts and retreat when you close the distance.

Dark Knight

Guard Area

Armored sentinels in dark steel, bound to guard specific passages. They won't chase far from their post but fight viciously within their territory.

Void Sentinel

Ranged & Teleport

Tall, thin void entities with a single glowing eye. They fire void bolts and teleport away when you get close — frustrating and dangerous.

Abyssal Herald

Patrol & Chase

Massive tentacled horrors from the deepest zones. Multiple eyes, gaping maw, writhing appendages. The most dangerous non-boss enemy in the labyrinth.

Cave Bat

Flying & Swoop

Erratic flying creatures that hang from cave ceilings. They swoop down in unpredictable arcs when you get close — hard to hit, easy to underestimate.

Fungal Spitter

Stationary & Ranged

Toxic fungi rooted to cavern floors. They launch poisonous spores in arcs at anything that moves. Stationary but deadly if you linger in their range.
